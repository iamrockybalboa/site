Discover Customer Personas & Workflows - Red Hat Systems Management

USER EXPERIENCE DESIGNER

I was tasked with identifying usability gaps in workflows of a new systems management application. A goal of this project was to delight key customers by anticipating their needs. The best way to map customer workflows is to interview each customer directly and aggregate the results as a persona. I then presented this data as personas which can be easily referenced by any team member. As a result it quickly became clear to each member of the team which parts of the exiting applications provide real value to customers.

Below you will find a summarized version of the processes and results.

Planning

RESEARCH

The success of the personas is directly determined by the accuracy of the data. By gathering info and forming relationships directly with customers guaranteed the best results.

Evangilization

The most critical step was to ensure each team member was familiar and comfortable with the personas. By making the personas easily consumable it made each team member well informed.

Benefits as a result of this effort

50%

HALF THE WORKFLOW WAS MISSING

We identified and built workflows which were missing from the current application.

30%

MORE EFFICIENT DEPLOYMENTS

We were able to identify and remove 30% of unnecessary existing workflows.

Sharing Personas with Product Teams

After the personas were documented it became important for the product teams to share and adopt the new personas. The best way to do that was to use the development team wiki.

Building a Company Profile

It was critical to understand not only who was managing the systems but what their infrastructure looked like. I built a company profile in the same wiki where I built the personas. This helped everyone understand the context of the personas above.
